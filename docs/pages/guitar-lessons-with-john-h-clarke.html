FAQ

Q: What if I've never played guitar before?

A: That's ok! No prior experience needed.

Q: What does "classical" mean in your teaching method?

A: The classical method is based on sight reading as a foundation for learning guitar, combined with proper technique and diverse repertoire spanning multiple genres.

Q: What do I need for in-person lessons?

A: Just bring your guitar! Lessons take place at Santa Fe Guitar Gallery, 328 S. Guadalupe St., Suite H, Santa Fe, NM 87501.

Q: What do I need for online lessons?

A: A chair with no arms, a computer or tablet with webcam and microphone, and a quiet space.

Q: Where is Santa Fe Guitar Gallery?

A: 328 S. Guadalupe St., Suite H, Santa Fe, NM 87501. Free parking is available on Montezuma Ave. You can also call 505-207-8977.

Q: What's your cancellation policy?

A: 24-hour notice required for rescheduling. Cancellations within 24 hours will be charged the full lesson fee.

Q: How often should I practice?

A: Even 15-20 minutes daily will show progress. I'll help you structure a practice plan tailored to your schedule and goals.

Q: What if I don't see a date/time that works for me?

A: Please use the contact form so we can discuss alternative options.
